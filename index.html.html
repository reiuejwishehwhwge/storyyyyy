THE THREE PIGS

‘The Three Pigs’ story is a tale all kids should know as it stresses a valuable lesson of life, i.e. hard work in the right direction will bear positive results. Although brothers, the three pigs possess different personalities, which comes across through the narrative as they set out to live their lives by themselves
